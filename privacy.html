Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At SmoothPark, we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Details: name, email address, phone number.
  • Vehicle Data: make, model, license plate and color for parked vehicles.
  • Payment Information: only stored as masked card details; full payment data processed securely through payment providers.
  • Usage Data: booking history, parking hours, locations visited.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize parking services.
  • To process payments securely.
  • For sending updates about bookings or account activity.
  • To improve our platform and user experience.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data except:

  • When required by law (e.g., legal obligation).
  • With your express consent.
  • To service providers (e.g. payment processors) under strict confidentiality.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. Please ensure you don’t share your account credentials or sensitive information externally.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time via your profile.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We’ll notify you of significant changes via email or prominent notification on our platform.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us.
